🎂 Communicake — Voice Clip Generator

This makes natural-voice (Piper) audio clips for every phrase, fully in your browser. Pick a voice, click Generate, wait, then download the zip. Unzip it into capacitor-app/www/audio/ (and your website's audio/), then rebuild.

Needs internet the first time (it downloads the chosen voice, ~20–60MB). After that the clips are bundled and the app works offline.
