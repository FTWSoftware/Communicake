Communicake — Voice Clip Generator

Makes natural-voice (Piper) audio clips in your browser, all in the Ryan US voice. It makes every clip the app needs — the buttons and pain/ice/number sentences, plus a full clip for each name & phrase combination (e.g. "I love you, David."). One click makes the lot.

The easy way (Chrome or Edge): click the big blue button, and when asked, choose your audio folder (or capacitor-app/www/audio). It writes every clip straight in and skips any already there. Then rebuild the app — done.

Needs internet the first time (it downloads the Ryan voice, ~20-60MB). After that it works offline.
